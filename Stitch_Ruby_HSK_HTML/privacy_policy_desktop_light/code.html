Privacy Policy

At Ruby HSK, we take your educational journey and personal privacy seriously. This document outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data to provide the best Mandarin learning experience.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Ruby HSK. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our students and users. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information that we receive through our platform, which includes our website, mobile application, and study tools.

By using Ruby HSK, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Our goal is to maintain your trust while delivering a world-class educational tool for HSK preparation.

2. Data We Collect

To provide personalized study plans and track HSK level progress, we collect several types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile preferences provided during registration.
  • Learning Data: Quiz scores, characters learned, daily streaks, and HSK mock exam results.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, device information, and platform interaction logs.
  • Usage Content: Notes, flashcards you create, and recorded speech for pronunciation practice.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to power the Ruby HSK experience, specifically for:

We use your data to create a custom curriculum that focuses on the HSK vocabulary and grammar points you find most challenging. Our algorithms analyze your past performance to schedule Spaced Repetition (SRS) sessions at optimal times.

Additionally, we use data to improve our platform’s UI/UX, ensuring that our "cute" and academic aesthetic remains both welcoming and functional for all age groups.

4. Data Security Measures

shield_lock Encrypted Protection

All sensitive learning data and account credentials are encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols during transmission and AES-256 at rest.

We restrict access to personal information to Ruby HSK employees, contractors, and agents who need that information in order to process it. These individuals are bound by strict confidentiality obligations.

5. Progress Tracking & Performance

A core feature of Ruby HSK is the ability to see your growth over time. To facilitate this, we store persistent records of your HSK Level assessments. These records are used to:

  • Generate personalized "Heat Maps" of your study consistency.
  • Predict readiness for official HSK certification exams.
  • Award merit badges and social rewards within our academic community.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have full control over your data. Under various global privacy regulations, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about your learning progress.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct any inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Erasure: Delete your account and all associated data permanently.
  • Data Portability: Export your vocabulary lists for use in other applications.
